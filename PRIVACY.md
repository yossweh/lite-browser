# Privacy Policy for LiteBrowser

**Last updated:** June 6, 2026

## Introduction

LiteBrowser ("we", "our", or "us") is a lightweight Android web browser application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

## Information We Collect

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do **not** collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- We do **not** track your browsing history
- We do **not** collect or store your bookmarks on external servers
- We do **not** require account registration
- We do **not** use analytics or tracking services

### Information Stored Locally
The following data is stored **only on your device** and never transmitted to us:
- **Bookmarks** — saved locally in the app's database
- **Settings** — your preferences (homepage, search engine, dark mode, etc.)
- **Tabs** — your open tabs (session only)

## Third-Party Services

### Advertising
Our app displays advertisements through the following third-party ad networks:
- **Adsterra** — Displays banner advertisements
- **Google AdMob** — Displays banner, interstitial, and rewarded advertisements (when available)

These ad networks may collect certain information for ad personalization and serving purposes. Please refer to their respective privacy policies:
- Adsterra: https://adsterra.com/privacy-policy/
- Google AdMob: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Website Content
When you browse websites using LiteBrowser, those websites may collect data according to their own privacy policies. We have no control over third-party website data collection.

## Permissions

LiteBrowser requests the following permissions:
- **INTERNET** — Required to load web pages
- **ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE** — Required to check network connectivity
- **WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE** (Android 9 and below) — Required only for downloading files

## Children's Privacy

Our application is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through our application. All data remains on your device.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
